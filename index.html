    Central Programming Club


    The development of my high school's first computer programming club has truly been a labour of love. Our first meeting saw an astounding 50 people, and we have been holding a steady count of about 20 students ever since (the rest we lost to the demands of homework). I have put in over 300 hours thus far, from building and maintaining our website to planning and leading club activities. I have tackled projects such as an introduction to code for beginners, a Turtle Graphics Tutorial, Browser Start Page Tutorial, and even helping to plan our own programming contest. What I am most proud of is facing my fear of public speaking to help bring together people with a passion for code and introduce it to others who would like to learn.
